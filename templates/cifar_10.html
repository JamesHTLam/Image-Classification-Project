CIFAR-10



Developed by Canadian Institute for Advanced Research (CIFAR), CIFAR-10 dataset is one of the most known teaching dataset for training image classification models.

It has 60,000 images of 32x32 pixel in 10 different classes of common objects. The 10 classes are airplanes, cars, birds, cats, deer, dogs, frogs, horses, ships, and trucks.

You can upload some photos in the following section, and then see the prediction result by pressing "Predict" button.
